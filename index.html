Understanding GEPCO's Online Payment Methods

To make payments easier for their customers, GEPCO has introduced several online payment methods:

1. Internet Banking

Many banks in Pakistan now offer internet banking services that allow you to pay your GEPCO bill directly through their online platforms. Users can log into their bank accounts, navigate to the bill payment section, and select GEPCO to proceed.

2. Mobile Banking Apps

Mobile banking apps like myABL, JazzCash, and Easypaisa enable users to quickly pay their bills through their smartphones. Here's how:

  • Download the app (if you haven't already).
  • Log in to your account and select the option for bill payments.
  • Choose GEPCO from the list, enter your reference number, and confirm the payment.

3. ATMs

You can also pay your GEPCO bills at automated teller machines (ATMs) that support bill payment services. Simply:

  • Insert your bank card.
  • Select the option for bill payments.
  • Choose GEPCO, enter your reference number, and follow the prompts to complete the payment.

4. Phone Banking

For those who prefer speaking to a customer service representative, GEPCO offers phone banking options via partnered banks. Call the customer service number of your bank, follow the instructions, and provide the necessary details to process your bill payment.

5. Third-party Payment Services

Various third-party services, such as EasyPaisa and JazzCash, offer partnerships with GEPCO that allow you to pay your bills easily via their platforms. You can visit their websites or mobile apps, select GEPCO as your payee, enter your reference number, and make your payment within minutes.

Key Takeaways

  • Always keep your 14-digit reference number handy for quick payments.
  • Utilize online banking apps and services for added convenience.
  • Familiarize yourself with the security measures of your chosen payment method to ensure safe transactions.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I pay my GEPCO bill online?

To pay your GEPCO bill online, log into your internet banking or mobile banking app, select bill payments, choose GEPCO, enter your reference number, and complete the transaction.

Can I pay my GEPCO bill using my phone?

Yes, you can use mobile banking apps like JazzCash and Easypaisa to pay your GEPCO bill anytime, anywhere.

Are there any additional charges for online payments?

Most methods do not require additional charges when paying through bank platforms; however, it's advisable to check with your specific provider.

Will I receive a confirmation of my payment?

Yes, you should receive a confirmation message or email after your payment has been successfully processed.

What if I encounter issues while making a payment?

In case of any issues, contact GEPCO customer service or the customer service department of your bank for assistance.
